## About Me

My name is Vojtěch Tóth and this is my personal web page. It's a little virtual place I dump my obsidian notes to. 

Check the [[Vault]] for my obsidian notes and [[Blog]] to read a post. I do these to exercise what I learned at my university. Currently, I'm pursuing a master's degree in computer science, focusing on artificial intelligence. I enjoy playing guitar, reading, going out or playing sports (hit me up if you play tennis).

Lately I've been working on a minimal torch-like framework for training neural networks from scratch. I already implemented enough to create a perceptron. Next step is to train a deeper network and test multiple optimizers on a MNIST dataset (like a true ML historian).
